关于我们

UNSW-CSA（新南威尔士州大学中国学生学者联谊会）是新南威尔士大学最大的学生社团之一，是澳洲大学中最大的华人学生社团之一。自1992年刚成立的不足50人，UNSW-CSA 现已发展成拥有3000余名活跃成员的大型社团，并在校园内以及社会外界弘扬中华文化上起到了显著的积极推动作用。社团为学生提供了可以在不同方面能力上得到锻炼的平台，同时经常组织各式各样的丰富课余活动，UNSW-CSA吸引了来自不同文化背景、不同学术领域的朋友们（主要成员来自中国大陆，香港，台湾，东南亚以及澳洲本土的华裔学生）。作为一个非盈利性的学生组织，我们没有任何宗教限制，我们的大门向任何人开放，因此不论你是想在大学中更多的了解中华文化还是想要认识更多的朋友，我们UNSW-CSA的每一位成员都随时欢迎您的加入。

UNSW-CSA is one of the largest international student societies at the University of New South Wales, at the same time, one of the largest Chinese student societies in Australia's universities. Since the establishment of UNSW-CSA in 1992, we have grown from a small society with less than 50 members to a society with over 3000 registered members in total. Our events provide a great social opportunity for those who are willing to meet people and make new friends, therefore we have a diverse range of members, pre-dominantly consist of students from Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, South Eastern Asia as well as local Australian Chinese. As a non-profit student organisation, we aimed at helping students in both academic and social life in Sydney. We denote our best effort to serve as a uniting force for students through which to promote a better understanding and identification with the Chinese culture and heritage. We do not discriminate any religious belief, thus if you are interested in Chinese culture or would like to have more friends during your tertiary study, we welcome you to join us.
